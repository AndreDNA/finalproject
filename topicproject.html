The Ethical Dilemma

If You Had The Ability To Hack Into The School's Computer System To Change A Grade Or Two, Would You?


If it where up to me and I wanted to save my skin or help out a dear friend, I probably would no matter the cost, because I would want to help out a friend in need anyway that I could. It may not exactly be legal to hack into the schools computers, but the fact that you want to go to a good school and you would do anything to get into one, it doesn't matter about the rationality of the situation.

I wouldn't know if all of my friends would do the same for me or not, but it doesn't matter because i don't ask anything in return except for their respect, like a true friend would give another.
